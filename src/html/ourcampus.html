Our Campus

At Holy Public Junior School, our campus has been thoughtfully designed to reflect our vision of nurturing young minds through a balanced and holistic learning environment. We believe a school is more than just a place for academics — it is a child's second home, shaping their values, character, and confidence. Students spend a significant part of their formative years within the school, and the atmosphere around them plays a vital role in their growth.

Understanding this deeply, we have built an environment that inspires curiosity, creativity, and all-round development. Every corner of Holy Public Junior School — from classrooms to play areas — is crafted to provide safety, comfort, and opportunities for exploration. Our modern infrastructure supports not only academic excellence but also emotional, physical, and social development, helping each student learn, grow, and shine.

Junior Classrooms

Bright, cheerful, and spacious classrooms enable children to stay enthusiastic during the day and study with sheer focus. Each element in our junior classroom is curated with the thought of young souls in mind. We ensure that there is proper air conditioning, ventilation and windows overlooking the greenery outside to keep the classes illuminated throughout the day. Exuberant and colorful designs empower students to not laze around and be involved with the teachers and fellow classmates. Our classrooms have enough seating to incorporate 40 students in each class without stuffing the space too much.

Senior Classrooms

Our senior classrooms are aesthetically designed to ensure that students from classes 6th to 8th are excited yet motivated to come to class daily. The airy classrooms have ample natural light, proper air conditioning, and enough space for students to move around and not feel confined. The classrooms also have bookshelves where students can keep their books if need be.

Labs

Our labs allow students to explore various fields of study by implementing theoretical knowledge into practical settings. At Holy Public Junior School, we believe that subjects cannot be solely understood through books, and it is essential for students to apply what they have learned in the classes by experimenting in the labs.

Science Lab

The Science lab allows students to experiment with different things that they learn in class and witness the magic of Science themselves!

Computer Lab

Digital experience is one of the most important things a student learns in school, and so our computer labs come equipped with the latest computers and software.

Math Lab

The Math lab is a place where students get to explore various patterns, puzzles, games, and practical learning materials to expand their ideation power.

Library

A Library is that one place where knowledge has no boundaries. From fiction, nonfiction, academic to non-academic books, the library is equipped with books across all niches to suit children of all age groups.

Sports Room

Physical activities are as important as academic learning. Our school facilitates a variety of outdoor and indoor games to cater to each student's interest areas.

Indoor Games

Our indoor game room is a vibrant, spacious, and fully air-conditioned area where students can enjoy games like chess, table tennis, and carrom.

Outdoor Games

We have a football ground, a cricket pitch, a volleyball court, and a badminton court, all spacious enough to accommodate players.
