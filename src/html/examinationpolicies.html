Holi Public School

PG(Play Group)

In Play Group, children are assessed through oral tests, interactive activities, and observation-based learning outcomes.

No formal written exams are conducted. The focus is on language development, recognition skills, and social growth.

Holi Public School

CLASSES Nursery, UKG and LKG

There is no test and examination, only learning outcomes are monitored.

CLASSES I – II

• For Primary classes, assessment is a continuous process.
• The academic year is divided into two terms.
• Students are assessed through:
• Class tests and worksheets
• Assignments and activities
• Term examinations (Half-Yearly & Annual)
• Equal importance is given to academics, handwriting, neatness, and participation in class activities.


CLASSES III – VIII

• Assessments and examinations follow a structured evaluation system as per CBSE guidelines.
• The academic year is divided into two terms. Each term includes:
• Periodic Assessments (PA) – Class tests, oral tests, projects, and activities
• Term Exams – Half-Yearly & Annual Examinations
• Both terms carry equal weightage in the final academic performance.

Examination Rules & Guidelines:

  1. No Leave During Exams
    • Students must be present for all examinations.
    • No leave will be entertained during exam days, except in unavoidable circumstances.
  2. No Re-Examination / No Change of Dates
    • Exams will not be pre-poned or postponed for any reason.
    • No re-examination will be conducted if a student misses an exam.
  3. Marking for Absentees
    • If a child misses an exam without valid reason, they will be marked absent and awarded no marks.
  4. Sports Representation
    • In case of participation at District / State / National level School Sports, the decision regarding exam marks will be taken by the Principal.
  5. Medical Absence
    • In case of illness, a Medical Certificate from a certified MBBS doctor must be submitted.
    • Marks / promotion decisions in such cases will be as per the examination department and school authorities.
  6. Authority & Final Decision
    • The Principal’s decision will be final and binding in all examination matters.
    • Parents cannot interfere in official examination policies.
