Admission Guidelines

  • Admission will be granted till the availability of seats on a first-come, first-serve basis. Submission/acceptance of the application form does not guarantee admission.
  • Student and parent interviews are mandatory for all applicants.
  • Special considerations/Priority will be given to applicants that have
    • Siblings already enrolled in Holy Public Junior School
    • Residing closer to the school
    • Children of school employees
  • Students with a physical impairment will be admitted provided that the environment can support their needs and that the family is willing to pay for any extra support if required or not provided for by the school.
  • Status of admission application to be provided to all the applicants.
  • Provisional/conditional grant of admission can be given in special cases after School Principal/Head of School/Management's approval.
  • Students can join at any time of the year provided that there is space in the intended grade.

Admission Procedure

  • Schedule a visit to the school to gain a better understanding of the school.
  • Participate in an assessment test conducted by the school to better understand the strengths and weaknesses of the student. Depending on the performance of this test, a scholarship may be provided.
  • Complete an informal interaction with the management of the school.
  • If you are selected, the school will offer provisional admission, and this status will be updated to confirmed admission upon the submission of the admission form and payment of the first-quarter fees.
  • Submit the required supporting documents to the admission team.
